About Me
Thanks for your interest, here is a quick story of me and this website. Hello my name is Simon Osaka. I am aged 56 and was born Oldham, UK. I am passionate about programming in JavaScript. I started my journey back in 1993 learning the C programming language and system analysis. Unable to gain work in I.T. I embarked on work in security. I worked as a door-supervisor, static security guard and retail guard at various locations around Greater Manchester.
In 2009 I renewed my interest in I.T. after completing a CISCO Technician's Certificate and ECDL. I began learning web development from books. I gained work in a Braile Workshop, initially as an Archivist for Hospital Records, later joining a team for creating an Encyclopaedia and Dictionary application aimed at helping Blind and Deaf people accessing I.T. education.
I worked later in Horticulture growing food crops and creating chilli hybrids for hotter chilli spices. I continued with HTML5, CSS3 and JS6 in my spare time, creating role play fantasy games to run in a browser. Also I began writing small questionairre apps, mainly to test my knowledge of web learning.
In the last year I have knuckled-down with my journey into web development. I really want to make a contribution to the development of I.T. from within the Uk, the country of my birth; by assisting other developers with the clarity of vision in changing the future via technology for the better.
Thank you for reading about my journey.
